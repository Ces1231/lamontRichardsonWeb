Genesee County Mobile Notary Services ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our mobile notary and fingerprinting services.

Personal Information

We may collect personal information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Name and contact information (phone number, email address, mailing address)

  • Document information and signatures
  • Fingerprint data for fingerprinting services
  • Payment information
  • Appointment scheduling information

Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect:

  • IP address and browser information

We use the information we collect to:
